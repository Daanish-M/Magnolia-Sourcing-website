What Our Process looks like:

Fabric Research

Our team is equipped with reading the fabrications and possess in-depth fabric knowledge, wash, finish and various types of aesthetics. Our partner suppliers are Tier 1 vendors offering diverse categories of sustainable fabrications including knits, denim and woven with vertical setup.

Garment Dev.

We have expertise on Speciality Women's, Men's and Children's wear using the correct fabrication and close sundries. Working in 3D CLO is available at our disposal. Proto-fit samples are developed while keeping in mind the bulk reproducibility process.

Sampling

Competitive and sustainable costing is key for long term business, options are worked out with suppliers keeping in mind the direct costing mechanism of supplier costing of each product. Costing needs are built up with a solid baseline costing product wise and category wise.

Costing

All volume runs for sampling are catered to in a quick turnaround time. Sample rooms for all needs are available. This ensures that the product is feasible and can be realistically produced. On time samples are key to bulk numbers and approvals for bulk production.

Bulk Production

Comprehensive product analysis meetings are done outlining any expected constraints within a product, the constraints are evaluated with clients prior to bulk. Bulk is executed at the highest quality standard utilising QC per approved pre-production sample.
